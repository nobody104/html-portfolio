The Best Movies According to Johan

My top 3 movies of all-time(in no particular order).

Mr. Nobody(2009)

This is a mind-bending exploration of choices and their ripple effects, Mr. Nobody is visually stunning and emotionally resonant.
Jared Leto delivers a soulful performance as Nemo, a man unraveling the "what-ifs" of life. The movie's non-linear narrative keeps you hooked,
blending philosophy, science, and romance into a dazzlingly unique experience.
It’s a film that makes you think about the infinite possibilities of existence long after the credits roll.
I think thus movie has changed my life greatly since I first saw it.

Joker

Joker is a haunting character study that turns the origin story into a gripping, raw, and unsettling masterpiece.
Joaquin Phoenix is phenomenal, embodying Arthur Fleck with such intensity that you can't look away even in the darkest moments.
The film is a brutal commentary on mental health, societal neglect, and the thin line between hero and villain.
It’s not just a movie—it’s an experience that crawls under your skin, literally...

Interstellar

Christopher Nolan’s Interstellar is a space epic with a soul, blending awe-inspiring visuals with an emotional core.
Matthew McConaughey’s heartfelt performance grounds the film, while the breathtaking depiction of black holes and distant planets is pure sci-fi magic.
At its heart, it’s a love story—about family, survival, and humanity’s enduring spirit to reach for the stars.
A must-watch for dreamers and science lovers alike.
